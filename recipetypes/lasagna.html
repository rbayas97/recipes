Lasagna

Description

A really good lasagna that is quick and easy to make. This is also a really good base for all meat specific sauces you would like to make in the future.

This recipe is great for beginners and to make for special occasions or when you want to ear Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • italian sausage
  • basil
  • ricotta

Steps

  1. Combine and cook ground beef and sausage for 10 minutes on m/h heat.
  2. Put tomato sauce into cooked meat. Cook on m/l heat for 45 minutes.
  3. You are done hooray
